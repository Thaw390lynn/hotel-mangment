PREMIER SUITE

At an expansive 95m², the well-appointed Premier Suite sleeps six and is an ideal option for a family break, offering plenty of space in which to chill out together and enjoy the ‘suite life’. Inclusive of breakfast and all-day refreshments, additional benefits include complimentary laundry service, TV room service ordering, on-screen billing info and TV video games. The Premier Suite also boasts panoramic city or park side views and provides access to our Premier Lounge, fitness center, swimming pool and tennis court on the terrace.

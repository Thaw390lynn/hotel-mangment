DELUXE ROOM

From 30m² in size, the spacious Deluxe Room comes in king-size or twin bed configurations and is equipped with a large ergonomic working area for attending to all your business matters. In addition, guests may enjoy complimentary access to our fitness center, swimming pool, tennis court on the terrace and children’s’ play area. The tasteful style of the Deluxe room is enhanced by the added benefit of a comfortable sofa, adding an extra level to your down time while you enjoy a calming view over the city of Yangon.
